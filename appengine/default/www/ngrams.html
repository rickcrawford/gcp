For autocomplete you may want to move on from just instant search - Bag of word models work great for this and have the added benefit of being a great source for you to analyze your catalog for SEO or related search purposes. Thankfully Google DataFlow can help you analyze these large datasets with ease.

This project has a sample dataflow projec that will analyze your corpus to ngrams of length 4. It also has the added benefit of performing this action at a category level. This allows you to bifercate your catalog by category to see what terms are most important for each root node of your taxonomy.

The source for this project is available here: https://github.com/rickcrawford/gcp/tree/master/dataflow/tokenizer
